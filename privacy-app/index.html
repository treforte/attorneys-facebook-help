Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data ClearSignal collects, how it is used in the login experience and marketing dashboard, and how to request deletion.

Effective date: March 2026

1. Who We Are

ClearSignal is operated by AlchemistMind to provide marketing attribution dashboards for agency clients.

Contact: admin@alchemistmind.pro

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data needed to operate the platform:

  • Account data: name, email, role, and client association.
  • Authentication data: encrypted password hashes and session metadata.
  • Ad performance data: campaign spend, impressions, clicks, conversions, and revenue metrics.
  • Integration credentials: API tokens needed to sync Google Ads and Meta Ads data.
  • Operational logs: sync status logs, error logs, and timestamps.

3. Login Dialog and Authentication Details

When you use the login dialog, your email and password are transmitted securely over HTTPS. Passwords are never stored in plaintext.

  • Passwords are verified against stored bcrypt hashes.
  • Access is role-based (e.g., SUPER_ADMIN, AGENCY, CLIENT).
  • Sessions are used to keep you signed in and authorize page/API access.
ClearSignal uses your account role and client mapping to restrict access so users only see data they are allowed to view.

4. How We Use Data

  • Display ad performance dashboards and attribution insights.
  • Sync campaign data from approved ad platform integrations.
  • Maintain platform security, monitoring, and reliability.
  • Support customer requests and troubleshooting.

We do not sell your personal or ad account data.

5. Data Sharing

We share data only with infrastructure/service providers required to operate ClearSignal (for example, hosting and database services), and only to the extent required for service delivery.

6. Data Retention

We retain account and analytics data while your account is active, or as needed for legitimate business and legal obligations. You may request deletion at any time.

Deletion instructions: data-deletion.html (or /data-deletion in the app route).

7. Your Rights

You can request to:

  • Access your stored account data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Revoke integration access tokens

Email requests to admin@alchemistmind.pro.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
