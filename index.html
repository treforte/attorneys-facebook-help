Frequently Asked Questions About Our Marketing Services

How much does paid ads management cost?

Paid ads management starts at $2,000/month with a one-time setup fee of $1,500 and a minimum ad spend of $5,000/month. That investment buys you dedicated management of your Google and Meta campaigns—audience strategy, creative testing, conversion tracking, and ongoing optimization—so you're not guessing or leaving performance on the table.

We've driven significant cost-per-lead improvements for clients in legal, home services, insurance, healthcare, and e-commerce. Pricing is transparent: no hidden fees, and we align our work with your goals and budget. Book a free strategy session and we'll outline exactly what you'd get for your spend.

How long until I see results from Google Ads or Meta Ads?

Most clients see measurable improvements within 30–45 days: better cost per lead, higher conversion rates, or more qualified traffic. We focus on quick wins (tracking, audiences, creative) while building toward long-term scale.

We back that with a clear guarantee: if you don't see a meaningful lift in the first 45 days, your second month is free. No long-term contracts—you can leave at any time. Our goal is to earn your trust with results, not lock you in. Past client results have varied; we'll set realistic expectations for your situation on our strategy call.

What is fractional CMO coaching and who is it for?

Fractional CMO coaching is strategic marketing leadership without a full-time CMO salary. You get an experienced strategist who audits your marketing, identifies growth opportunities, and gives you actionable plans—ads, CRM, landing pages, positioning—so you can scale without the cost and commitment of a senior hire.

It's ideal for founders and owners who are ready to grow but don't have a marketing leader in-house, or who want an outside perspective. We offer three tiers: Monthly ($1,500), Twice Monthly ($2,850), and Weekly ($4,000). All include strategy analysis, action plans, and direct access so you can make decisions with confidence.

Does Alchemist Mind Pros work with law firms and attorneys?

Yes. We've run paid ads and built marketing systems for Sweet James Accident Attorneys, The Siegel Law Group, and other law firms. We understand compliance, audience targeting for legal (estate planning, personal injury, family law), and how to fill seminars and consultation calendars efficiently.

Our work for attorneys has included lower cost per lead, faster seminar fills, and CRM/automation so leads don't slip through the cracks. Results we've seen for other firms are not a guarantee for every practice; we'll assess your market and goals on a free strategy call and tell you exactly how we'd approach your firm.

We serve B2B, D2C, e-commerce, lead generation, and local service businesses. That includes legal, home services (HVAC, plumbing, roofing), healthcare and medical practices, insurance and Medicare (e.g. Chapter), professional services, and e-commerce brands.
