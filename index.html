Common Questions

Before You Book a Call

Does ClearSignal replace my existing CRM?+
No. ClearSignal connects to the CRM you already use — HubSpot, Lead Docket, and others — rather than asking you to migrate your case data somewhere new. It's the attribution and intelligence layer sitting on top of what you have, not a rip-and-replace.
What tools will this let us cancel?+
It depends on your current stack, but most firms find overlap with standalone call-tracking subscriptions, separate intake/form tools, and the manual hours spent building reports by hand. We'll map your specific stack against ClearSignal on the demo call and show you exactly where the overlap is.
How is client data handled and secured?+
Client and lead data is encrypted and handled with the same care as the case management systems you already trust. Specific compliance and security details for your firm's requirements are something we'll walk through directly on a call, since every firm's needs are a little different.
How long does setup actually take?+
It depends on which integrations your firm needs, but most firms are fully connected within 2-3 weeks. Since ClearSignal is already running in production for real firms, you're not waiting on us to build the core platform — just configure it for your sources.
Can I try before I commit?+
ClearSignal and AI Sales Coach are demo-only — both are wired into your specific ad accounts, CRM, and call data, so a generic sandbox trial wouldn't actually show you anything real. CheckinApp, though, is lightweight enough to try free for 14 days with no setup fee.
What does the AI Sales Coach actually do?+
Every recorded consult or intake call is automatically scored against a fixed rubric — rapport, discovery, objection handling, value articulation, and close — with a plain-language coaching note on what a top closer would've said differently. It also tallies your most common objections and surfaces which ones your team handles well versus where deals are being lost.
Is this only for estate planning and personal injury firms?+
Those are where it's been built and proven first, but the underlying system works for any consult-based professional service — anywhere a lead has to turn into a booked appointment and a booked appointment has to turn into a signed client.
What happens on the demo call?+
A real walkthrough, not a sales pitch — we'll look at your current setup, show you exactly what ClearSignal would surface for your firm, and give you a straight answer on pricing and timeline. You leave with clarity either way.
