Data Deletion Instructions

If you have connected your Facebook account or ad data to ClearSignal and would like your data removed, follow the steps below.

How to Request Data Deletion Required by Meta

ClearSignal uses your Meta ad account data solely to display performance metrics in your dashboard. We do not sell, share, or use your data for any other purpose.

To request complete deletion of your data from our systems:

1
Send an email to admin@alchemistmind.pro with the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
2
Include your Meta Ad Account ID and the email address associated with your ClearSignal account
3
We will confirm receipt within 48 hours and complete deletion within 30 days
4
You will receive a confirmation email once all your data has been permanently deleted
Send Deletion Request

What Data We Delete

Upon receiving your request, we will permanently delete:

Your Meta ad account performance data (spend, impressions, clicks, conversions)
Campaign and ad set metrics stored in our database
Your access tokens and authentication credentials
Your ClearSignal account and profile information
All sync logs and historical data associated with your account

Note: Deleting your data from ClearSignal does not affect your data within Meta's own systems. To manage data on Meta's platforms, visit your Facebook Settings.

Automatic Disconnection

You can also revoke ClearSignal's access to your Meta account at any time by visiting your Facebook app permissions settings:

1
Go to Facebook Settings → Security and Login → Apps and Websites
2
Find ClearSignal Ads and click Remove
3
Then follow the email steps above to delete your stored data
